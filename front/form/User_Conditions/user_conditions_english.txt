By submitting your registration request as a volunteer to “Au Temps Donné”, you accept the following terms and conditions:</p>

<ul>
     <li><strong>Volunteer commitment:</strong> By becoming a volunteer with “Au Temps Donné”, you undertake to provide your support and contribution to the activities of the association in accordance with its objectives and mission.</li >
     <li><strong>Respect for the rules and values of the association:</strong> You acknowledge and agree to respect the rules, values and code of conduct of the association, and to comply with these standards in all your interactions with the association and its beneficiaries.</li>
     <li><strong>Confidentiality:</strong> You understand and agree to maintain the confidentiality of sensitive information to which you may have access as part of your volunteering, and not to disclose this information without prior authorization from the association. </li>
     <li><strong>Responsibility:</strong> You agree to take responsibility for your own actions as an association volunteer, and to behave responsibly and ethically at all times.</li>
     <li><strong>Safety:</strong> You agree to take the necessary steps to ensure your own safety and that of others while volunteering, and to follow the occupational safety and health guidelines provided by the association.</li>
     <li><strong>Engagement in activities:</strong> You agree to actively participate in the activities and projects of the association to the extent of your abilities and availability, and to inform the association in the event of unavailability or inability to participate in a planned activity.</li>
     <li><strong>Duration and termination of the commitment:</strong> You understand that your commitment as a volunteer is voluntary and that you are free to end this commitment at any time by informing the association of your decision.</li>
     <li><strong>Image rights:</strong> You consent to the association using your image (photographs, videos) as part of its communication and promotional activities, unless otherwise indicated on your part .</li>
     <li><strong>Training and personal development:</strong> You recognize the importance of your continuing training as a volunteer and you undertake to participate in the training and personal development activities offered by the association.</ li>
     <li><strong>Consent to Conditions:</strong> By submitting your application to register as a volunteer, you confirm that you have read, understood and accepted the terms and conditions of volunteering described above.</li>
</ul>
